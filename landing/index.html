FAQ

Common questions

Is this data scraped without permission?

StoreScope only indexes publicly available product data via Shopify's open /products.json endpoint — the same data any browser can access. We comply with robots.txt, respect rate limits, and offer instant opt-out for any merchant who wants their store removed.

How often is the data updated?

The full index is refreshed weekly. Trending snapshots are recorded with each pipeline run, so historical trend data accumulates over time. Individual store lookups via the free X-Ray tool hit the live Shopify API instantly.

How does product clustering work?

We compute a perceptual hash (pHash) of each product's primary image. Products with a Hamming distance of 8 or less are grouped into the same cluster — meaning they share a visually identical supplier image even if the title or price differs across stores.

Can I request my store be removed?

Yes. Send a request to optout@storescope.com with your .myshopify.com domain. Your store will be removed from the index within 48 hours and excluded from future crawls.

Do you cover all Shopify stores?

Currently 1,400+ stores with ongoing expansion. The crawl targets stores with public product catalogs discovered via web archives and public Shopify store directories.
