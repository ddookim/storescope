FAQ

Still on the fence?

How do I access my account after payment?

Right after payment, you'll receive a confirmation email with your dashboard link and access credentials. Open it and you're in — no technical setup required. Manage or cancel your subscription any time via the Paddle billing link in that same email. Questions? Email support@storescope.com.

Where does StoreScope's data come from?

StoreScope only indexes publicly available product data via Shopify's open /products.json endpoint — the same data any browser can access. We comply with robots.txt, respect rate limits, and offer instant opt-out for any merchant.

What is the Trend Score?

The Trend Score (0–100) combines week-over-week store adoption rate, total store count, and growth velocity. A score of 80+ signals a product cluster gaining strong momentum this week. It updates weekly with every pipeline run.

How often is the data updated?

The full index is refreshed weekly. Trending snapshots accumulate over time, building your historical trend library. Use the free X-Ray tool on this page to check any public Shopify store's trending products on demand.

Is monitoring competitor stores legal?

Yes, completely. StoreScope only reads publicly listed product pages — the same pages any customer sees when they visit a Shopify store. We don't access private data, bypass authentication, or scrape anything hidden. We comply with robots.txt and offer instant opt-out for any merchant.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel at any time from your Paddle billing portal, no questions asked. Cancel during the 7-day trial and you won't be charged. After cancellation, your access stays active until the billing period ends.

Can I request my store be removed?

Yes. Email optout@storescope.com with your .myshopify.com domain. Your store will be removed within 48 hours and excluded from future index runs.
