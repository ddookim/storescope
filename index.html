FAQ

Common questions.

01 Product & data
What exactly is in the Monday digest?

A ranked list of every product gaining adoption across 1,400+ Shopify stores this week. Each row shows: product name, total store count, week-over-week growth %, price spread (min/median/max across stores), and Trend Score 0–100. Filter to Score 80+ and you have your sourcing shortlist in 30 seconds.

What is the Trend Score and why should I trust it?

The Trend Score (0–100) combines three things: how fast the product is being added to new stores week-over-week, the total store count, and the overall growth rate. A score of 80 or higher usually means strong momentum that week. It's not a subjective rating — the score is calculated from real store data refreshed every Monday.

How often is the data updated?

The full index refreshes weekly, every Monday 06:00 PST. Trending snapshots accumulate over time, building your 30-day (Starter) or 90-day (Pro) trend history. Use the free X-Ray tool above for on-demand lookups any day of the week.

How is this different from other product research tools?

Most research tools track what's already trending on ad networks. By that point, the winners are usually saturated. StoreScope reads adoption directly from Shopify storefronts, so you see products spreading 1–2 weeks earlier from a different angle.

02 Trust & compliance
Is monitoring competitor stores legal?

Yes, completely. StoreScope only reads publicly listed product pages via Shopify's open /products.json endpoint — the same data any browser sees. We don't bypass auth, don't access private data, comply with robots.txt, and offer instant merchant opt-out.

I'm a merchant — can I request my store be removed?

Yes, instantly. Email optout@storescope.com with your .myshopify.com domain. Your store is removed within 48 hours and excluded from all future index runs. No questions asked.

03 Billing & account
What if I don't see value in 7 days?

Cancel from your Paddle billing portal any time during the trial — you're not charged. After billing, we also honor a 14-day refund window, no questions asked. Email dodo@storescope.com and the refund is processed same day.

Right after checkout, you receive a confirmation email with your dashboard link and login credentials. No technical setup. Manage or cancel via the Paddle billing link in that same email. Questions: support@storescope.com.
